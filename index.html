December 3, 2021

Hi everyone! This is the beta version of the ‘Bloggy Vloggy’ written blog that I’ll slowly work and build upon. In other words, this website is a home for my thoughts!

You guys have been familiar with my content in the short-video format on Instagram stories and Snapchat -- my infamous use of the Rio De Janeiro filter and so forth. Having a written and (I’m hoping) heartfelt passage or journal entry should be a good change of pace. Plus, I’m still creating content in a different way by also building the website from scratch. Using mostly HTML and CSS, as well as Github pages to generate this website, it’ll be a good starting point to this journey I’m about to embark on.

Just to dive a little deeper into my thoughts, and simply put, I’m trying to break into the tech industry and make that $BANK. More importantly, I could say that I enjoy coding. The pursuit of anything is always going to require passion. I’m also a believer of the growth mindset, and this industry will prove how resilient I am to that belief because of the never-ending updates to all programming languages.

Furthermore, I had just completed a coding bootcamp and I’m pretty invested into this industry. My thinking is that there’s a silver lining through it all. I already enjoy making content, so now let’s add some code on top of it.

Throughout the journey, I hope to gain insight, feedback to what you guys think I can improve on. The famous saying once goes, “There’s light at the end of the tunnel.” To be honest, I like the saying “What’s done in the dark shines in the light” a bit more. Hard work never goes unnoticed, and that wraps it up for this first blog post!
